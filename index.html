Who We Are

The Open Coalition is a loose affiliation of organisations, all working in the open space. Originally founded in partnership by Wikimedia UK, Open Knowledge, Creative Commons and Mozilla, the Coalition has grown to include many other organisations. We believe that by working together, across organisational boundaries, we can help each other to work more effectively, and continue to build a free and open Web.

What We Do

  • Facilitate better communication across the sector
  • Work with partners to build joint projects
  • Grow our network by identifying and partnering with open organisations
  • Encourage the understanding and use of open practices across the Web
